This version of the popular game <b>Tic Tac Toe</b> for the Android platform lets you test your skills against the wits of the robot. Put three <b>X</b>-marks in a line horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before the computer can line up three <b>O</b>-marks and you win. Also <b>Tic Tac Toe</b> contains three different difficulty levels: Easy, Medium and Hard.
